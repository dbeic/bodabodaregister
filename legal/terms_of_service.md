## 4. QR PIN PROTECTION

### 4.1 Purpose of QR PIN
- Protects your member information from unauthorized access
- Ensures only authorized individuals can verify your membership
- Gives you control over who accesses your QR code data
- Prevents unauthorized verification of your membership status

### 4.2 PIN Creation and Management
- **Initial Setup:** You must create a 4-digit PIN during registration
- **PIN Change:** You can change your PIN using your current PIN and National ID
- **Admin Reset:** Administrators can reset your PIN after verification
- **Security:** PINs are stored as encrypted hashes, never in plain text

### 4.3 QR Code Encryption
- Your QR code data is encrypted using your PIN
- Anyone scanning your QR code must enter the PIN to view your details
- The QR code shows a lock icon to indicate it is PIN-protected
- Verification attempts are logged for security

### 4.4 Consequences of PIN Misuse
- Sharing your PIN may result in unauthorized access to your information
- You are responsible for all activities using your PIN
- Report compromised PINs immediately to administration
- Delayed reporting may result in liability for unauthorized access
