### 4.2 Data Usage

## 5. BADGE ISSUANCE AND USE

### 5.1 Badge Generation
- Badges are generated upon successful member registration
- Each badge contains a unique QR code for verification
- Badges are the property of Busia County Bodaboda SACCO

### 5.2 Badge Use
Members must:
- Present their badge when requested by authorities
- Keep their badge secure at all times
- Report lost or stolen badges immediately
- Not alter or reproduce badges without authorization

### 5.3 Badge Verification
- QR codes may be scanned by authorized personnel
- Verification confirms membership status
- Verification does not store personal data unless logged
